About

At Peach, we’re building more than an app. We are on a mission to change how patients and caregivers interact with their health.

“
Many of the 250,000 deaths in America attributable to medical error each year can be traced to poorly co-ordinated care. With data at their fingertips, common standards to enable sharing and a strong incentive to get things right, patients are more likely to spot errors.
”
 - The Economist

At Peach Health, we care deeply about:

data ownership & accuracy

  • Healthy data is data which is not outdated, and with an accurate record, comes a healthier life.
  • Truly own your data through the convenience of your phone and avoid stashes of paper and old school faxes

security & privacy

  • We use end-to-end encryption to ensure your data is safe.
  • We never share data with 3rd parties without your explicit consent.

empowering the patient

  • Easily share complete medical histories with the touch of a button.
  • Know before it’s too late; never forget an immunization or appointment.

streamlining the process

  • Enjoy easy access to your all of your data on the go at any time.
  • Track everything related to your child’s growth, health, and development all in one place.

hi, my name is sam, and this is why I started peach.

When I was 16 I visited a new doctor in NY, and they asked me for my immunization history. A relatively simple question that I could not answer without texting my mother. Together we searched for hours through papers and folders to dig up my medical history.

Having lived in more than 5 countries by the age of 18, it was incredibly difficult to track my health data. This is why I created Peach. Hopefully you too will find it useful.
